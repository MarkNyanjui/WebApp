Assassin's Creed Odyssey

What is it?

Assassin's Creed Odyssey is an action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft and released in 2018. Set in ancient Greece during the Peloponnesian War, you play as a Spartan mercenary on an epic journey across a massive open world filled with history, myth, and conflict.

Gameplay

Players explore a huge map, fight enemies in real-time combat, sail the Aegean Sea, and make choices that affect the story. You can upgrade your character with different skills and abilities as you progress through the game.

Setting

The game is set in 431 BCE across ancient Greece, featuring iconic locations like Athens, Sparta, and dozens of islands. The world is brought to life with real historical events, characters, and Greek mythology woven into the story.
